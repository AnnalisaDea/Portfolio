who I am

HELLO

I'm a 22 year old UX Designer. I come from a small town near Trento, in Trentino-Alto Adige. Last year I graduated in "Communication Interfaces and Technologies" at the Department of Psychology and Cognitive Sciences of Rovereto. During this course of study I acquired knowledge about User Research, User Evaluation and Prototyping.

I am currently attending the two-year course in Digital Design & Communication at LABA, Brescia. The aim of the two-year course is to create specialised professionals who know how to operate in all business communication scenarios, synthesising a marketing objective into a creative communication idea, managing its dissemination and involving users.
